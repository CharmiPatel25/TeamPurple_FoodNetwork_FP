RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups blueberries
  • 1 cup blackberries
  • 1 cup raspberries
  • 1/2 cup strawberries
  • 1/2 cup large fresh figs
  • 1-ounce dark chocolate shaved
  • 1/4 cup dried mulberries, optional
  • 1 cup homemade almond, cashew, or oat milk

PREPARATION METHOD

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. On a large baking sheet lined with parchment paper, pour all fruit on top with a dash of cinnamon and pinch of sea salt, stir to evenly spread.
Bake for 20 minutes or until warm and sugars start to release from the fruit. Take out of the oven and sprinkle optional garnishes and dark chocolate shavings on top. The heat from the fruit will start to melt and warm them both. Serve in a small bowl, pour milk on top as needed/desired. Enjoy!
